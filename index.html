About

I am an aspiring web developer with a background in sales and big data analysis. Whether it's making sales material to present to clients or creating data dashboards for company stakeholders and clients, I've always been passionate about visually communicating information through good design. After having worked at an e-commerce tech giant in Japan for 3 years and at a market research tech company in Silicon Valley, I was inspired to switch to a career in web development. I started off building simple websites on my own, and from there started a course in web development at General Assembly to transition into this new career path.

Projects

Kioku

A flashcard app

I came up with the idea of building a Japanese/English language flashcard web app because this app would be a good starting point for a Japanese/English dictionary app that I would like to build in the future. As for this flashcard app, the vision is to have it be used by people who would like to memorize Japanese words and short phrases via repetition.

The back-end was built with Ruby on Rails, while front-end was built with JavaScript, Handlebars, and Bootstrap. I also Implemented use of a Text-to-Speech API to add audio of the Japanese pronunciation to each word.
